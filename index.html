The Rise of Streetwear in Hip Hop Culture

Julius Martin

Nov 18, 2022

Feat.
Layzie L.U.

Pop Culture

What do you think has been the driving force behind the recent surge in popularity of high-end streetwear fashion?

I've always been fascinated by how streetwear has become such a powerful force in popular culture. Movies, music videos, television, it's the new high-end. I love seeing it incorporated into music videos, like Cardi B's "I Like It" and Ariana Grande's "7 Rings," and I'm constantly amazed by how high-end fashion designers are incorporating streetwear elements into their collections.
